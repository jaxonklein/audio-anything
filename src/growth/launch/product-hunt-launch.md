Hi everyone! Jackson here.

I built Readcast because I was drowning in articles. I subscribe to 12+ newsletters, follow 50+ blogs, and have 2000+ saved articles in Pocket. I wanted to listen to them, but existing text-to-speech sounds like a robot reading a Wikipedia article. Not great.

So I spent the last 48 hours building this: paste any URL (articles, blog posts, PDFs), pick from 4 podcast-quality voices, and get instant audio. Works on the web, works offline, syncs across devices if you sign up.

**What's included:**
- Free: 3 audio generations per hour (expires in 1 hour)
- Premium ($4.99/month): 200 generations/month, permanent library, sync

**The tech:**
- Claude for smart content extraction (skips paywalls, handles PDFs)
- ElevenLabs for human-sounding narration (not robot TTS)
- Built for speed: generation takes ~30 seconds

3. What features would make this 10x better?
